Frequently Asked Questions

Is Glovia free to use? +
Yes! Glovia has a free tier with access to many lessons. We also offer a premium subscription for advanced content.
What age group is Glovia for? +
Glovia is designed for elementary and middle school students from grades 1 to 8.
